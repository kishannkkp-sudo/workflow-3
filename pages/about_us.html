About FirstJobTech

Welcome to FirstJobTech, your number one source for all things related to IT Job Updates, Career Guidance, and Recruitment News. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of job opportunities, with a focus on dependability, authenticity, and uniqueness.

Our Mission

Founded in 2024 by Kishan Prajapati, FirstJobTech has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for "Helping Freshers Start Their Career" drove us to do tons of research on the job market, so that FirstJobTech can offer you the world's most verified and latest private job updates. We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

What We Do

  • Verified Job Updates: We strictly verify every job posting to ensure it is from a legitimate source/official career page.
  • Career Guidance: We provide tips on resume building, interview preparation, and skill development.
  • Fresher Focus: Special attention to entry-level roles to help new graduates find their footing in the industry.

We hope you enjoy our updates as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
